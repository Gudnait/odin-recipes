Peanut Soup Recipe

Description

Bolivian Peanut Soup is a hearty and flavorful dish that warms the soul. It features a rich peanut-based broth, often made with roasted peanuts blended into a smooth paste. This creamy base is combined with a variety of vegetables, such as carrots, potatoes, and sometimes noodles, as well as tender pieces of beef or chicken. The soup is seasoned with spices like cumin and oregano, creating a complex and satisfying flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • Peanuts
  • Beef or chicken
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Bay leaves
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable broth
  • Noodles (optional)

Steps

  1. Roast the peanuts until golden brown and fragrant.
  2. Blend the roasted peanuts with water to create a smooth peanut paste.
  3. Sauté onion in a pot until softened.
  4. Add the beef or chicken and brown on all sides.
  5. Pour in vegetable broth and add carrots, potatoes, cumin, oregano, bay leaves, salt, and pepper.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the meat is tender and the vegetables are cooked.
  7. Stir in the peanut paste and simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the soup reaches your desired thickness.
  8. If desired, add noodles and cook according to package instructions.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro or parsley.
